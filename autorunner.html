Auto Runner Game

Available on itch.io

This is an endless auto-runner, obstacle course game I made with the inspiration of some game ads where the player controls the avatars left or right to choose the right mathmatical equations and avoid the obstacles.

There were some game ads on the internet that looked really fun but the actual game looked different. So, I thought it would be cool to make that kind of games by myself. In this game, the player can only move left or right and they will always automatically move forward. There will be some mathematical equations that the player needs to choose from, in order to increase the troop count. There are also some obstacles and enemies to be avoided. The game will get harder as time goes on.

During the development of this game, I learned to make simple models in blender and import them into my game. I used a website called mixamo for rigging the characters and animating them. To make the game more interesting, I learned about ragdoll system and made my little guys die when they collide into an obstacle or an enemy. It was a fun experience overall. I wish to be able to model more stylized characters in the future. The game is available on itch.io
